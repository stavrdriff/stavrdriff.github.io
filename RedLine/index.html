THE REDLINE SOLUTION

The Contract Model Solution

Contracts are an essential part of doing business - and also one of the biggest risks. Do it yourself or hire counsel? Either way, if you’re using a Transaction Approach, you’re solving the wrong problem.

You’re taking greater risk with each deal, have no understanding of how the current deal compares to your ‘standard deal’ let alone how one deal compares to another, and your paying too much for each transaction - in dollars, risk, or both.

RedLine Solutions helps you move beyond the transaction. We help you build a contract model for your business - reducing the time, cost, and risk associated with your deals.

Readiness

We analyze your Contract Readiness by reviewing your contract artifacts and processes showing you what you have and what you need.

MARQup

We create a model of your ‘standard’ business terms - the terms that matter most to your business - using our MARQUp tool and build out the templates and processes you need.

Deal Support

We actively negotiate and close deals for your business using your model and the contract artifacts and processes created to make sure the system works.

Analyze

We score each deal as it compares to your model and create a ‘heat map’ - to identify areas of risk, opportunity, and concern.

Track

We help you drive consistency across all your deals by tracking deal scores over time and iterating on the model based on deal outcomes.
