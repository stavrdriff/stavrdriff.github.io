Scaled Pricing

Building a contract ecosystem is a process. We support you all along the way. Pay only for what you need - we scale to fit.

Readiness

We recommned starting here. Knowing what you have helps you identify what you need. We take a look at your current contract artifacts and processes - perform a gap analysis to see what’s missing - and then we help you fill the gaps.

Model

We help you identify the key terms and positions that drive value for your organization. Using MARQUp - our SAAS tool for deal analysis - you can compare your deal to the model you created to identify hidden risks and opportunities in your deals that are important to you.

Deal Support

We don’t just help you build your contract ecosystem, we help you run it. We provide deal support services from drafting deals to negotiating and closing them.

Analyze

MARQUp let’s you compare and analyze a deal to your model so you can’t forget a term that’s important to you. And with the ‘heat-map’ view and scoring feature, you’ll immediately see the deal terms that are most risky to you. MARQUp also lets you run ‘what-if’ scenarios so you determine the most effective changes for your deal.

Track

MARQUp stores your deal scores over time allowing you to see measured improvement in your contracting postions. MARQUp also shows you how multiple deals compare with the same party over time so you can iterate learnings back into your contracting process.
